How to Donate

You may donate to the animal shelter in several ways: By sending a check or money order to our U.S. office, by wiring funds to our U.S. bank account, by using a credit card through the on-line Pay Pal system, or by shipping donated supplies to our U.S. office. All donations in cash or kind are tax-deductible to you.
 
The Afghan Stray Animal League is a federally tax-exempt organization under Section 501 c 3 of the Internal Revenue Code. It is based in Arlington, Virginia. It has an unpaid board of directors but no paid employees or officers in the United States.

Other Ways to Contribute


We also welcome contributions of any light and portable animal care supplies, especially Front Line or Advantix flea medicine, Heartguard heartworm medicine, Drontal deworming medicine, nylon leashes and collars of all sizes, small and medium plastic cat and dog carriers, plastic travel bowls with cage attachments, hair brushes and combs, rawhide dog chews in sealed plastic pouches, 3 cc and 60 cc plastic syringes, large latex surgical gloves (sizes 8 and 8 ½,) 10 -and 15-foot-long cable tie-outs with clips, and KMR powdered kitten and puppy formula in cans. No dog or cat food, please!!! All portable supplies may be shipped to the Arlington office (please email us for the address), and they will be hand-delivered to the shelter every three months. 

Cash donations are used to pay for operating expenses and medical supplies. Our shelter's operating costs are approximately $3,000 per month, including rent for the main shelter/clinic and a separate boarding building, local staff salaries, and basic supplies. A contribution of $100 pays for 10 rabies vaccines or two spay operations. A contribution of $500 pays for one month’s rent for the main shelter. Money is raised separately for the shipping of any animals to adopters’ homes. Tax-deductible receipts will be mailed immediately to the donor. Thank You!!
